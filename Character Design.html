The characters are intentionally designed with exaggerated body proportions, such as long arms, slender builds, and pointed chins. This style was inspired by the Chinese sci-fi comic 《末日保护者》 (Mo Ri Bao Hu Zhe), which humorously uses stretched and dramatic anatomy similar to the visual exaggeration seen in certain scenes from Attack on Titan. I enjoy fantasy, supernatural, and slightly eerie themes because they make everyday life feel more interesting and imaginative. That is why my characters take on fantastical forms, such as water ghosts, sirens, and other monster-like fantasy creatures. Their slightly 3D appearance comes from adding subtle shading and “pop-up” details, as I enjoy creating semi-realistic elements while maintaining a stylised illustration approach.
