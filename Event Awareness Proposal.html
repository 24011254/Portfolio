Design → Event Awareness Proposal



The background is about an E-waste factory, since the event wants the audience to visit an e-waste factory.

There are elements of electronic devices, such as a TV and two laptops, because the event is about E-waste.

The wavy blue lines represent electricity and data transmission, which are relevant to electronic devices.

There is emphasis because there is a focal point, the “E-Waste Factory Visit,” which is the largest font size and lets the reader focus on the heading. Next, there is also an emphasis due to the contrast between the blue and black-and-white colours, which enables the reader to focus on the blue areas. Also, emphasise the blue areas that make the message necessary and stand out. And also, the blurred background makes the electronic devices, blue wavy lines, and messages stand out.

There is proportion because the sizes of the electronic devices and the blue wavy lines are small to large, from the furthest to the nearest, which makes sense.

The wavy blue lines' flow creates rhythm, guiding the viewer’s eyes from top to bottom.

There is a sense of unity because all the design elements, such as TV, laptops, and the blue wavy lines, are in 2D design. Additionally, the whole picture shared similar colours, which are white, grey, black, and blue, which creates a sense of harmony.
