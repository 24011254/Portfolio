Gestalt Principles:

Figure and Ground:
The “black” man is the figure, and the futuristic background is the background, because the man stands out from the city.

Similarity:
There is a similarity between “CONFIDENCE” and “FUTURE” because both are in neon style.

Proximity:
Those sentences that are grouped are related to one another.

Principles of Design:

Contrast:
Bright neon text pops out from the dark background

Alignment:
Centred layout creates a symmetrical balance

Hierarchy:
Huge and colourful words hook attention first

Emphasis:
Bright yellow button stands out to call user for action
