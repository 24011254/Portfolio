Design → LTA Overhead Bridge Design


This artwork was created for LTA, based on the theme “Walk, Cycle, Ride.” Because the theme contains three key actions, I divided the composition into three sections: walking at the top, cycling in the middle, and bus travel at the bottom. These scenes represent everyday movement in Singapore, such as students returning from school, people cycling in parks, and commuters taking buses. The intention was to make the artwork relatable to pedestrians who use the stairs at Woodlands South, showing real-life travel happening in the same direction. I aimed for simplicity and clarity, without fantasy elements, so the illustration remains grounded and easy to understand.
