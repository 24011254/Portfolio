Immersive Technologies → VR Escape Room


Concept
A horror-themed VR escape room built in Unity with VR Foundation. The player takes the role of Nicholas, a kidnapped victim, who must solve puzzles inside a house that mirrors the traumatic childhood home of the antagonist, Vincent. The story background explains Vincent’s psychological trauma and motives, which drive the dark atmosphere of the game.

Genre & Style
Horror, mystery, emotional storytelling with an eerie art style. Fear is created through disturbing visuals (blood, crime scene hints) and background screaming/crying audio.

Game Loop
Explore room → Find clues → Solve puzzle → Repeat → Escape.

Gameplay Scope

  • 10 house-like rooms
  • 4 story-based puzzles
  • Focus on clue discovery and object interaction in VR

Puzzles Overview

  1. Key Puzzle – Find a hidden key and place it near the door. Clue word “Key” appears in the room.
  2. Gem Puzzle – Touch a gem in the master bedroom; it disappears when solved. Clue “Gem” is displayed on the wall.
  3. Button Puzzle – Press a small cabinet button in the living room to stop a disturbing noise. Clue “Button” is near the main door.
  4. Lamp Puzzle – Click a table lamp to turn off the light. Clue “Lamp” appears on the TV.

Unique Selling Points

  1. Every puzzle links to story meaning
  2. Combination of horror, mystery, and emotional depth
  3. Interactive VR puzzle objects

Target Audience
Teenagers and adults who enjoy VR escape rooms.

Core Features

  • VR interactive objects
  • Clue-based puzzle solving
  • Environmental storytelling

Development Plan (Milestones)
Week 4–11: Story & puzzle design → 3D assets → VR coding → Testing → Art polish → Playtest → Bug fixing → Final polish.

Platform
Mobile VR using Unity + VR Foundation.
